Welcome to Multiplication Madness

In this game, you will act as a waiter.

  • Listen to the Customer's grouped order in English.
  • Drag and drop the correct food items onto each specific **plate**.
  • Calculate the total price correctly to practice your multiplication groups.
  • Chat with the customer in English to keep them happy!
  • Tip: Click an item on a plate if you want to remove it!
